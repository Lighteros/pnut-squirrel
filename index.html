Peanut’s Life Cut Short

Beloved Peanut the Squirrel was taken from his home at P’Nut’s Family Freedom Farm by the NYSDEC and euthanised. His sudden loss shook his fans to their core, sparking an outcry against his ruthless treatment.

Tragedy Sparks Outrage

Peanut’s upheaval and euthanisation served as a wake up call for many. Why should the Government be allowed to break into your home and kill your pet? His death triggered a huge demand for reform alongside a presidential election.

A Symbol of Rescue

For seven years, Peanut was an online sensation, showcasing his innocence and playful nature in countless videos. He was more than just a squirrel, he was everyone’s squirrel, a symbol of second chances and hope.

Forever in Our Hearts

Though Peanut is no longer with us, his spirit lives on in the hearts of those he touched. His memory will continue to inspire kindness, compassion, and action. Peanut forever.

His Memory Lives On

Peanut will never be forgotten. He inspired many in life, and countless others with his untimely death. His legacy serves as a reminder to fight for our freedoms, so that we may protect the ones we hold dearest.

PEANUT. REST IN PEACE.
